A simple, manual M-Pesa payment process.

How to Pay

  1. Choose a plan from the cards below.
  2. Go to your M-Pesa menu and select **"Send Money"**.
  3. Send the exact KES amount to this number:
    0742455932
  4. After paying, **send a WhatsApp message** to the *same number* (0742455932) with:
    1. Your **M-Pesa Confirmation Code** (e.g., `SJL123ABC`)
    2. Your **Account Email:** `{{ current_user.email }}`
  5. Credits will be added to your account manually once the payment is verified (usually within 24 hours).
